Pricing options


Wave Acoustics Simulator is available to use via a purchased license.


Whether you are an educator, architect or consultant, please take a look at our licensing plans.


We offer two plans, one suited to educational institutions such as universities while the other is suited to commercial users such as those in industry, consultancy and architecture. Industry licensed software do not display a watermark in the animation files.


Both plans include two types of licensing: annual and perpetual. Perpetual licenses grant the user the right to use the software forever, including free maintenance and support during the first year of the license. After the first year, maintenance and support services should be purchased. On the other hand, annual licenses have the duration of 12 months and should be renewed before that time period should the user choose to keep using the software. Annual licenses also include one year of free maintenance and support.
