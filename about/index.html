About the Wave Acoustics Simulator

The Wave Acoustics Simulator is a leading 2D simulation tool that focus on visualization and rapid prototyping. It is designed to bring acoustics to architects, planners, educators and students alike.

About the author

Dr.Nuno Ferreira graduated from the University of Salford with a MSc Environmental Acoustics. He later obtained his PhD from the University of Liverpool in numerical Vibroacoustics. He then moved to Portugal and has been working as a software engineer since.
